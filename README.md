> AI-Powered Multi-Agent Service Platform for Customer & Employee Support

## Problem Statement

Organizations often struggle with fragmented customer support, HR, finance, and technical services spread across multiple systems. This leads to slower response times, duplicated work, and poor user experience.

Synova addresses this challenge through an AI-powered multi-agent platform that understands user requests, routes them to the appropriate specialized agent, retrieves relevant knowledge, and automates support workflows from a single interface.

---

## Headline Result

- AI-powered multi-agent platform
- Customer & Employee Support
- Intelligent Intent Classification
- Knowledge Retrieval (RAG)
- HR, Finance, Sales & Technical Agents
- Modern Full-Stack Architecture
- Multi-tenant Workspace
